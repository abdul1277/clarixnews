Frequently Asked Questions
How do I submit a news tip?+
Use the contact form above and select "News Tip or Story Idea". Include as much detail as possible — what happened, where, when, and any sources or evidence you have. All tips are treated confidentially.
How do I request a correction?+
Select "Correction Request" in the form above and explain what you believe is inaccurate and why. Include a link to the article. Our editorial team reviews all correction requests and responds within 12 hours.
How can I advertise on ClarixNews?+
Select "Advertising Inquiry" in the contact form. We offer display advertising, sponsored content, and newsletter sponsorships. Our advertising team will send you a media kit with audience data and pricing.
Can I republish ClarixNews content?+
All ClarixNews content is protected by copyright. For republication rights or licensing, please contact us via the form above with subject "Press & Media Inquiry". Short quotes with attribution are permitted under standard fair use.
How do I subscribe to the newsletter?+
You can subscribe using the newsletter form on our homepage or any category page. We send a morning briefing every weekday — a curated summary of the most important stories, completely free.
